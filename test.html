1. Progress bar
    • How far it is filled and how many hours are left show how well the player has understood the game
  2. Scoreboard
    • A number given for correctly reusing, reducing, and recycling.
    • A higher number will be measured to indicate learning of how to follow the three r’s
    • Also to measure students understanding of the definitions of the three R’s
  3. Bubble answer after reusing
    • Relate points to knowledge
    • Assess students’ capability to integrate knowledge to real life
    • Assess students’ understanding of the definition behind reusing
  4. Surveys
    • Assess students' understanding and attitude towards conservation and what they can do.
