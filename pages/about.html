A little more about me

I am on a journey to learn and apply how to create
better user experiences and to design engaging
lessons in the digital world.

I am currently studying Digital Media Design for
Learning at Steinhardt, NYU since summer 2021.
This program is constantly upgrading my
perception and my work by learning about various
design principles like design thinking, human
cognition, user experience, digital skills, etc.

Before finding my way into digital design, I worked
as a French teacher and a business analyst in India.

I am also a Research Assistant at Steinhardt, NYU.
